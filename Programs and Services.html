Programs and Services

Comprehensive care addressing every aspect of a child's development

Our Core Programs

Education Program

Quality education tailored to each child's learning needs and abilities. We provide academic support, tutoring, and mentorship to ensure educational success.

  • Quality instruction in all core subjects
  • Individual tutoring and academic support
  • Homework assistance and study groups
  • Vocational training for older youth
  • College preparation programs

Residential Care

Safe, comfortable, and nurturing living environment designed to make children feel at home. We provide proper nutrition, healthcare, and daily living support.

  • Safe housing in well-maintained facilities
  • Nutritious meals and dietary support
  • Healthcare and medical services
  • Hygiene and personal care education
  • Life skills training

Counseling & Therapy

Professional mental health services providing emotional support, trauma-informed care, and therapeutic counseling for healing and emotional growth.

  • Individual counseling sessions
  • Group therapy and support groups
  • Trauma-informed care
  • Anger management programs
  • Coping skills development

Recreation & Activities

Fun and engaging activities that promote physical health, creativity, social skills, and personal interests while building confidence.

  • Sports and physical fitness
  • Arts and crafts programs
  • Music and drama activities
  • Outdoor and nature activities
  • Hobby clubs and interest groups

Family Reunification

Where appropriate, we work to reunite children with their families through counseling, mediation, and support programs that strengthen family bonds.

  • Family counseling services
  • Parental support programs
  • Supervised visitation
  • Transition planning
  • After-care support

Youth Development

Comprehensive programs focused on developing leadership skills, self-confidence, and preparing youth for independent adulthood.

  • Leadership training
  • Career exploration programs
  • Entrepreneurship workshops
  • Life skills training
  • Independent living skills

Child Placement Plan

🎓

Education

Children attend pre-school and Junior High at Fountain of Life Academy.

🏥

Health

Medical cases are managed at Prudent Cottage Center with fully funded referrals.

🍎

Food

Well-balanced, nutritious meals and fruits provided daily from our institutional kitchen.

🏠

Shelter & Care

Airy rooms, comfortable bedding, and full clothing/uniforms provided as needed.

🤝

Psychosocial Care

Dedicated social workers provide guidance, counseling, and external referrals.

⚽

Recreation

Full playgrounds, media access, and organized tours for children's exposure.

⛪

Spiritual Growth

Christian practice and pastoral guidance are integrated into daily life within the premises.

👨‍👩‍👧

Family Contact

Regular school-break visits and free interaction with friends and next of kin.

📜

Behaviour & Policy

Positive reinforcement and proactive guidance; physical punishment is strictly prohibited.

Age Groups Served

Early Childhood
(4-7 years)

Preschool and early elementary education, socialization, play-based learning, and foundational skill development.

School Age
(8-12 years)

Comprehensive academic support, recreational activities, counseling, and programs focused on building self-esteem and friendships.

Adolescents
(13-17 years)

Advanced academics, career preparation, leadership training, counseling support, and preparation for independent living.

Young Adults
(18-21 years)

Independent living skills, higher education support, employment training, and gradual transition to adult responsibilities.

Specialized Support Available

We provide culturally sensitive, trauma-informed care with specialized programs for children with special needs, disabilities, and unique circumstances.

How Our Programs Make a Difference

Education Excellence

Our educational programs help children catch up academically, discover their talents, and envision a successful future. We've helped countless children progress from failing grades to honor rolls and college admission.

Emotional Healing

Through professional counseling and therapeutic services, we help children process trauma, develop healthy coping mechanisms, and rebuild trust in adults and relationships.

Personal Development

Our recreation and youth development programs help children discover their passions, build confidence, develop leadership skills, and prepare for independent adulthood with hope and determination.
