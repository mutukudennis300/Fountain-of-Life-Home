Bishop Dr. Florence Mbai

The Journey of a Visionary:

Bishop Dr. Florence Mbai

A visionary leader, mother, and spiritual pillar of Fountain of Life Deliverance Ministries.

The Academic Foundation

Bishop Dr. Florence Mbai’s leadership journey is rooted in a tradition of excellence, beginning with her formative years at Machakos Girls' High School. This early foundation of discipline and academic rigor prepared her for a life of high-impact leadership and spiritual service.

Bridging Corporate Strategy and Divine Purpose

Before her full-time consecration to ministry, Dr. Mbai navigated the complexities of the corporate world. As the Deputy Managing Director at Fountain of Life Safaris, she honed the strategic planning and organizational development skills that now distinguish her global ministry. This unique blend of corporate wisdom and spiritual depth allows her to lead with both executive precision and a shepherd’s heart.

A Mission Born in the Streets

Her heart for the vulnerable was first ignited in the streets of Pumwani and Eastleigh. It was here, amidst the marginalized, that she discovered her true calling to restore hope to the hopeless. Today, that local mission has blossomed into a global platform, yet her focus remains unchanged: transforming lives through the power of faith.

Educational Depth and Legacy Building

An alumna of the Heritage Theological Seminary, Bishop Mbai is committed to raising a generation of empowered, ethical leaders. As the spiritual pillar of the Fountain of Life Children’s Home, she provides a home, a future, and a foundation of faith for those who need it most.

Global Spiritual Leadership

As the visionary leader of Fountain of Life Deliverance Ministries, Bishop Dr. Florence Mbai has expanded her reach beyond borders. Through international summits and a growing global congregation, she continues to mentor leaders and transform lives worldwide.
"Restoring hope to the hopeless and building a legacy of faith for the next generation."
— Bishop Dr. Florence Mbai
