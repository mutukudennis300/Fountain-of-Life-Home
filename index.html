OUR HISTORY & JOURNEY

Our journey began in 2001, not as a fully-fledged institution, but as a simple feeding program in Pumwani. What started as a gesture of compassion soon became a calling.

We witnessed boys as young as eight living on the streets... They had no safe place to sleep, no school to attend, and no one to call family.

We reached where we are today through the unwavering support of our community and the resilience of the children who call this place home.

Why "Fountain of Life"?

Just as a fountain provides life to the land around it, we aim to be the source of stability and joy for children who have faced hardship. Our mission is to break the cycle of poverty and abandonment through faith, education, and unconditional love to the Boy child.
