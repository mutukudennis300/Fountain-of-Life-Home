Privacy Policy

Your privacy and the protection of our children is our highest priority

1. Introduction

Fountain of Life Children's Home ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and maintaining the safety and confidentiality of our children. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect information.

Last Updated: April 29, 2026

2. Child Protection & Photo Policy

We take the safety and privacy of our children seriously.

  • Parental Consent: We only share photos of children with explicit written consent from their guardians or legal representatives.
  • Identity Protection: Where possible, we use photos that show children from the side or back to protect their identities. Children's full names are not published with photos.
  • Limited Use: Photos are only used for organizational purposes (reports, donors, educational materials) and never sold or shared with third parties.
  • No Facial Recognition: We do not use facial recognition technology on photos of children.

3. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact Information: Name, email, phone number, mailing address
  • Donation Information: Payment details (processed securely through authorized payment processors)
  • Website Usage: IP address, browser type, pages visited (via cookies)
  • Child Information (Internal Only): Educational records, health data (stored confidentially)

4. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Process donations and maintain donor records
  • Send important updates and newsletters (with your consent)
  • Improve our website and services
  • Respond to inquiries and provide customer support
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Support child welfare and educational programs

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Secure SSL encryption for all transactions
  • Restricted access to sensitive information
  • Regular security audits
  • Secure storage of all child and donor records

6. Third-Party Information Sharing

We do NOT share your personal information with third parties except:

  • Payment processors (for secure donation processing)
  • Legal requirements (law enforcement, court orders)
  • With your explicit written consent

7. Children's Online Privacy Protection

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children online without parental consent. If we become aware that a child has provided information without appropriate consent, we will take immediate steps to delete such information.

8. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can control cookie settings in your browser preferences. Disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

9. Your Communication Preferences

You have the right to:

  • Opt-out of promotional emails (click "unsubscribe" link)
  • Request access to your personal information
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for photos and communications

10. External Links

Our website may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review the privacy policies of any third-party websites you visit.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We encourage you to review this page regularly. Continued use of our website implies acceptance of updated terms.
